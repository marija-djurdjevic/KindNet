# 🤝 KindNet - Volunteer Management Platform

[![.NET](https://img.shields.io/badge/.NET-5C2D91?style=for-the-badge&logo=.net&logoColor=white)](https://dotnet.microsoft.com/)
[![Angular](https://img.shields.io/badge/Angular-DD0031?style=for-the-badge&logo=angular&logoColor=white)](https://angular.io/)
[![C#](https://img.shields.io/badge/C%23-239120?style=for-the-badge&logo=c-sharp&logoColor=white)](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/csharp/)

**KindNet** is a comprehensive web platform designed to streamline the organization, coordination, and management of volunteer activities. Developed as a Bachelor's thesis project, this system connects volunteer organizations, individual volunteers, and socially responsible companies into a single, cohesive ecosystem.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🏢 For Volunteer Organizations
* **Event Management:** Effortlessly create, schedule, and manage volunteer events.
* **Volunteer Coordination:** Track applications, assign roles, and manage volunteer schedules.
* **Evaluation & Feedback:** Evaluate volunteer performance after events to ensure quality and dedication.
* **Statistics & Analytics:** Generate detailed reports on event success, volunteer turnout, and overall organizational impact.

### 🙋‍♀️ For Volunteers
* **User Profiling:** Build a comprehensive volunteer profile showcasing skills, interests, and past experiences.
* **Event Discovery:** Browse and apply for upcoming volunteer opportunities that match personal interests.
* **Rewards System:** Earn points, badges, or certificates based on participation and evaluations to gamify and reward the volunteering experience.

### 💼 For Companies (Sponsors)
* **Resource Allocation:** Companies can register and donate resources (materials, funds, venues) to specific organizations or events.
* **CSR Tracking:** Track the impact of their corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Backend:** C# / .NET Framework
* **Frontend:** Angular
* **Architecture:** RESTful API design ensuring scalable and secure communication between the client and server.
